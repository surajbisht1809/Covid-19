COVID-19 Global Impact

Covid-19 has affected everyone's lives around the globe, below chart shows impact level on various regions and countries. Following scatter graph plots total confirmed-cases with total reported deaths and highlights most impacted countries Countries in all the regions are impacted with few countries including US, Brazil, India and Russia have reported most numbers of confirmed cases. United kingdom (UK) and Mexico have highest confirmed case-fatality ratio. UK, US and Mexico has highest death percentage per 100K population.
Apart from few exceptions latest trend shows rapid growth in confirmed cases, deaths and daily new cases across all countries. China total cases, deaths and daily new cases looks stagnant, Oceania region is least impacted possibly population factor.
